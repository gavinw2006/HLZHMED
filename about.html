Our Story

Who We Are

Beijing Hongling Zhihe Medical Technology Co., Ltd. (HLZHMED) is a professional medical technology enterprise established and operating in Huairou District, Beijing, China.

We are dedicated to bridging the gap between advanced medical science and practical healthcare applications. Our multidisciplinary team brings together expertise in biomedical engineering, clinical research, technology consulting, and device distribution.

From healthcare IT system development to pioneering stem cell and gene therapy research, HLZHMED offers a comprehensive suite of services designed to support medical institutions, research organizations, and healthcare enterprises in China and beyond.

  • Registered and operating in Huairou District, Beijing
  • Comprehensive medical technology service portfolio
  • Focus on innovation in stem cell & gene therapy
  • Authorized Class I & II medical device distribution
  • Committed to ethical, patient-centered medical advancement
